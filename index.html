About CMC

Our Mission

The Club des Métiers de la Construction (CMC) at University of Constantine 3 is a platform for architecture and construction students to develop their skills, network with professionals, and contribute to the built environment of Algeria.

We organize workshops, site visits, competitions, and networking events to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and professional practice.
